Edward's Osaka Castle Walks · Pre-Tour Companion

Three men. One castle. Sixty years that shaped Japan forever.

Oda Nobunaga

The warlord who cleared the ground. Spent eleven years trying to destroy the fortress that stood here before.

Toyotomi Hideyoshi

The peasant who built it. Rose from nothing to become the most powerful man in Japan. Osaka Castle was his statement to the world.

Tokugawa Ieyasu

The survivor who buried it. Waited thirty years, then destroyed everything Hideyoshi built — deliberately and permanently.
